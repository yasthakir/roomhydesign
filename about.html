About Roomhy

Simplifying student accommodation, one room at a time.

Our Mission

To be the most trusted and student-friendly platform for finding hassle-free accommodation, empowering students to focus on their education and personal growth by providing safe, verified, and affordable living spaces.

Our Story

Roomhy was built by students, for students. We personally experienced the chaos, uncertainty, and stress of finding a reliable place to live while moving to a new city for education. Brokerage fees, unverified listings, and lack of transparency were common hurdles.

We knew there had to be a better way. That's why we created Roomhy – a platform dedicated to simplifying the search for student homes. We're more than just a listing site; we aim to be your first friend in a new city, helping you find a comfortable and secure space where you can truly thrive.

Our Core Values

Trust & Safety

Prioritizing verified listings and secure processes.

Student-Centric

Putting student needs and well-being first.

Transparency

Providing clear information and zero brokerage.
